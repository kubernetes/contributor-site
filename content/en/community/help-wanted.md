### Not sure where to start?

- **[Attend New Contributor Orientation](/docs/orientation/)** held monthly, no experience needed.

- **[Join #kubernetes-new-contributors](https://kubernetes.slack.com/archives/C09R23FHP)** on Slack.
- **[Check the Resilience Dashboard](/community/resilience/)** to see which projects need help most.
